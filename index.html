About Me

Hello! I am Sandeep Kumar Samota, currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Mathematics at the National Institute of Technology, Rourkela. My research focuses on poverty analysis using artificial intelligence, specifically targeting the KBK regions of Odisha. I am passionate about using technology to address socio-economic issues and aim to contribute valuable insights through my research.

PhD Project

My Ph.D. project involves the application of artificial intelligence to analyze and address poverty in the KBK (Kalahandi-Balangir-Koraput) regions of Odisha. By leveraging machine learning algorithms and data analysis techniques, I aim to identify key factors contributing to poverty and suggest effective interventions. The ultimate goal is to create data-driven solutions that can help improve the living conditions and economic opportunities in these regions.
